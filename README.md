# Corvexa

> **Built by Builders. Governed by Community. Powering the Future of a Sovereign Layer 1.**

Corvexa is a community-first Web3 ecosystem born on Solana and launched through Pump.fun, created by developers who believe real value comes from shipping code, building products, and empowering open communities. More than a token, Corvexa represents a shared movement toward a sovereign, EVM-compatible Layer 1 where governance, innovation, and ownership belong to contributors rather than centralized organizations.

Designed for builders, validators, creators, and long-term supporters, Corvexa serves as the economic and social layer connecting today's Solana ecosystem with tomorrow's decentralized infrastructure. The project's public messaging describes it as a Solana community asset supporting the development of a sovereign, EVM-compatible Layer 1. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}
